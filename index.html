Who We Are

Our mission is to advocate for companies to stop replacing their workers with AI. Instead, we aim to promote AI-coexistence: not replacing workers with AI but instead augmenting them with AI. It is to that extent that we let ourselves be known and to drive regulatory change to protect workers across all industries.

We come from different backgrounds and experiences, all the way from tech to even the arts, but we are all driven by the same ideology: to keep our jobs in the coming age of AI automation.

What Our Aims Are

  • Raise Awareness Share stories, data, and resources that highlight why change is needed.
  • Mobilize Communities Organize local and global actions that empower people to take part.
  • Influence Policy Advocate for policies that safeguard our job securities and ensure entry-level positions exist for the new generation to enter.
  • Build Connections Create networks of support, collaboration, and shared learning.
